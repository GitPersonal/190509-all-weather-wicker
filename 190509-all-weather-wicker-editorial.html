Since 1962, we’ve been your wicker destination.

What’s so special about ours?

Highly skilled artisans hand-weave durable vinyl (synthetic wicker) over rust-resistant metal frames to create pieces that will stand the test of time. Legs of chairs, settees and other pieces are hand-wrapped to make our furniture more durable.

Variety of weaves

Our furniture is hand-woven in three classic styles.

shop all wicker-furniture

Single

The tightest weave for added durability, it’s used in our Bari Collection, as well as our colorful Del Rey chairs.

Open

Light and airy with a vintage feel, it’s often combined with the other two styles. It’s used in our Celeste and Swingasan® Collections.

Double

The most popular weave for its durability and stylish sensibility, it’s found in our Casbah and Echo Beach Collections.
